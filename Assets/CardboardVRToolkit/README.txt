
Cardboard VR Toolkit (v1.0)

Includes:
- StoryBubble.cs
- ScenePortal.cs
- VRButton.cs
- ReticleInteractableBase.cs

How to Use:
1. Drag the CardboardVRToolkit folder into your Unity project's Assets folder.
2. Create prefabs from the scripts as described in the guide.
3. Assign your audio, scenes, or UnityEvents in the Inspector.
4. Ensure all interactables have colliders and are placed on Layer 8.
5. Use with CardboardReticlePointer for gaze interaction.

Happy building!
